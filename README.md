# Analysing_Blinkit_Data_Excel
**blinkit Grocery Data Excel Project**

**Project Overview**

This project uses the blinkit Grocery Dataset to perform detailed sales and performance analytics across multiple outlets.

The goal is to extract actionable insights into product performance, outlet efficiency, and sales distribution using advanced Excel-based analysis and dashboarding.

Through data cleaning, transformation, and visualization, this project builds a structured foundation for understanding how blinkit’s grocery operations perform across various item categories, outlet types, and geographic tiers.

________________________________________
**Dataset Description**

It captures item-level sales information across multiple store formats and includes product, outlet, and rating details.:

•	Sr No: Unique ID for each entry

•	Item Identifier: Code for each grocery product

•	Item Type: Product category (e.g., Dairy, Snacks, Beverages)

•	Item Fat Content: Regular or Low Fat classification

•	Item Weight: Average product weight (kg)

•	Item Visibility: Shelf display ratio in store

•	Sales: Total revenue (₹) per item

•	Rating: Customer satisfaction score (1–5)

•	Outlet Identifier: Store code

•	Outlet Establishment Year: Year outlet was opened

•	Outlet Type: Store classification (Supermarket / Grocery)

•	Outlet Size: Store size (Small / Medium / High)

•	Outlet Location Type: City tier (Tier 1 / 2 / 3)

________________________________________
**Business Objectives**

The primary goal of this analysis is to evaluate blinkit’s retail performance across products, stores, and locations to support data-driven business decisions.

Specific objectives include:

•	Analyse sales and product performance across different item categories and fat contents.

•	Evaluate outlet performance based on type, size, and location.

•	Understand the relationship between item visibility, weight, and sales.

•	Identify key trends in customer ratings and product demand.

•	Create an interactive Excel dashboard for management to visualize KPIs and regional performance in real time.

________________________________________
**Key Business Questions**

**1. Sales and Performance Analysis**

•	What is the total sales performance across all outlets and how does the average sales per item vary by category?

•	How do different outlet sizes (High, Medium, Small) impact total revenue?

•	Which outlet establishment years show the strongest growth and peak sales performance?

•	Are sales distributed evenly across outlet types (Supermarket vs. Grocery Store)?

**2. Product & Category Insights**

•	Which item types (e.g., Fruits & Vegetables, Snack Foods, Household, Dairy) contribute most to overall sales?

•	Which low-selling categories need promotional strategies (e.g., Seafood, Breakfast, Starchy Foods)?

•	How does fat content (Regular vs. Low Fat) influence overall sales distribution?

•	Which product segments have the highest opportunity for improvement in visibility-to-sales ratio?

**3. Outlet & Regional Performance**

•	Which outlet locations (Tier 1, 2, 3) drive the most sales volume?

•	How does outlet type (Supermarket Type1, Type2, Type3, Grocery Store) affect total and average sales?

•	Are Tier 3 outlets generating higher total sales due to larger customer bases or broader product availability?

**4. Customer Experience & Rating Trends**

•	What is the average customer rating across outlets, and how does it relate to total sales performance?

•	Do higher sales correspond to higher customer ratings, or are there outlets with sales–rating imbalance?

•	How can BlinkIT leverage high-rated but low-selling items to boost visibility and profits?
